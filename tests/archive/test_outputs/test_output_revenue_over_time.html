FY 2024 Performance
Revenue for FY 2024 demonstrated a robust upward trajectory, increasing from $125,000 in Q1 to $178,000 by Q4, marking a total growth of 42.4%. Notably, the most significant surge occurred between Q3 and Q4, highlighting a strategic boost in market demand and operational efficiency. This strong performance not only reflects the effectiveness of our recent initiatives but also positions us favorably for sustained growth in the upcoming fiscal year, emphasizing the need for continued investment in our key growth areas.
